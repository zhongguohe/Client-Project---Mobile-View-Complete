Welcome to the UMSI Career Development Resource Hub

Your Central Hub for Career Success at UMSI

The UMSI Career Development Resource Hub helps students quickly find trusted career resources in one place. Whether you are exploring possible career paths, preparing for internships and jobs, building your application materials, or learning how to network more effectively, this site is designed to help you get started with confidence.

Instead of scrolling through long lists of links, you can use this site to move directly into key topics, discover popular resources, and connect with the Career Development Office for more personalized support.

Why Use This Resource?

  • Everything in one place: Quickly access career resources for resumes, networking, events, coaching, and more.
  • Student-focused organization: Resources are grouped by topic so it is easier to know where to begin.
  • Trusted support: Links and guidance are based on UMSI Career Development Office materials and services.
  • Accessible on any device: The site is designed to work well on phones, tablets, and larger screens.
  • A clearer starting point: Popular resources and major topic areas are highlighted so students can take action faster.

How This Homepage Helps Students Get Started

Find resources faster

Major career topics are highlighted on the homepage so students do not need to dig through long, text-heavy pages before finding the right starting point.

See what is most useful

The Most Popular Resources section gives students a quick entry point into commonly used tools, guides, and services.

Connect with support

Students can move from self-service resources to direct CDO support when they want personalized coaching or guidance.

Connect with the UMSI Career Development Office

The UMSI Career Development Office offers personalized support through career coaching, workshops, events, and online resources. You can explore more services, make use of career preparation materials, and learn how to take your next step with guidance tailored to UMSI students.
